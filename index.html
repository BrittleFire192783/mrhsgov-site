                  What it is

                  A timeline that opens a card with facts, the core question, the holding, reasoning, and why it matters. Long term goal: AP Gov plus APUSH in one place.

                  How to study

                  • Browse the thumbnails to get the era flow.
                  • Use Quick jump for an exact year.
                  • Read Quick Take and Holding first.
                  • Use Connections to link themes.
